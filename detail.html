项目总结

我设计并且迭代了触宝运营平台（简称TOP）。包括设计师端：皮肤编辑、标签图上传、数据统计；运营端：皮肤审核，产品上架、生成报表。我发现三个需要注意的方面： 1. 效率 2. 专注 3. 引导 这些方面通过多次用户访谈和反馈得出。然后依据设计原则，针对特定的场景，通过交互设计和视觉设计解决上述问题。

Project Summary

I designed and iteration the TOP ( short for TouchPal Operation Platform) . Include designer end: theme edit, data review, sticker edit; OPM end: theme auditing, production stock, report generation. As it turns out, I found 3 major points we shoud notice: 1. Effiency 2. Focus 3. Guide I draw these conclusion from times of User interview and feedback. After that, I focus on aesthetics, every piece of detial.
